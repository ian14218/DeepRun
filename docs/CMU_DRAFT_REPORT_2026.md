## The Awards

### The "I Knew Before Everyone" Award -- Phil50
Darius Acuff Jr. at pick #4 (22.9 PPG, 4-seed Arkansas). While everyone was tripping over themselves for 1-seeds, Phil50 said "give me the guy who actually scores" and grabbed the highest PPG player in round 1. The man averaged nearly 23 points a game and Phil got him 4th. Bold. Calculated. Possibly genius.

### The "PPG Chaser" Award -- Phil50
With an average PPG of **14.1** across all 16 picks, Phil50 drafted the most productive roster on paper. He also went deep into Cinderella territory with Dominique Daniels Jr. from **13-seed California Baptist** (23.2 PPG) and Cruz Davis from **13-seed Hofstra** (20.2 PPG). Either these guys are going to score 40 in a first-round upset win and Phil looks like a prophet, or they're going home Thursday night and Phil's left holding the bag. No in-between.

### The "AJ Dybantsa Sweepstakes Winner" -- Newman1623
The projected #1 NBA draft pick fell all the way to pick #30. Newman1623 snagged **AJ Dybantsa** (25.3 PPG on a 6-seed BYU). That is the highest PPG player in the entire draft and he got him in ROUND 3. The rest of the league is going to be sick when Dybantsa drops 35 in the Round of 64.

### The "Blue Blood Collector" Award -- Voltron46
Average seed of **3.1** -- the lowest in the league. Voltron46 stacked their roster with Purdue (x2), UConn, Arizona, Duke, Michigan, Virginia, and Florida players. This is the "play it safe, ride the favorites" strategy. If the top seeds hold chalk, this team prints money. If March gets Marchy... well.

### The "Tbeach Tax" Award -- Tbeach1306
22 MINUTES to make pick #2. Twenty-two. The draft started at 11:11 PM and Tbeach didn't make their second pick until 11:33 PM. The entire league was sitting there, phones in hand, wondering if Tbeach fell asleep, lost wifi, or was consulting a panel of NCAA analysts. Yaxel Lendeborg better be worth the collective hours of everyone's life that were lost.

### The "Speed Demon" Award -- HogNuts69
Two seconds. HogNuts69 took exactly **2 seconds** to draft Chase McCarty (pick #155). Either they had their board locked in or they were panic-clicking. Also took 3 seconds on Aday Mara. When you know, you know.

### The "Evan Nelson Enthusiast" Award -- Scouter10
Pick #140: Evan Nelson from Arizona. **0.6 PPG. 3.5 minutes per game.** This man has scored fewer points this season than most of you scored on your SATs. He plays three and a half minutes a game. That's barely enough time to take off your warmups. Scouter10 really looked at the entire remaining player pool and said "yeah, give me the guy who averages half a point." In fairness, he's on a 1-seed, so if Arizona goes to the Final Four, Evan Nelson will be there. Sitting on the bench. Cheering really hard.

### The "Mr. Irrelevant" Award -- Devin Royal (Scouter10)
The last pick of the draft -- pick #160 -- was Devin Royal from Ohio State (13.7 PPG, 8-seed). Actually a pretty respectable Mr. Irrelevant? 13.7 PPG is higher than half the players drafted in rounds 1-3. Scouter10 may have accidentally won the end of the draft by not overthinking it.

### The "Chaos Agent" Award -- Newman1623
Average seed of **5.8** -- the highest in the league. Newman scooped players from Hofstra (13), Furman (15), Texas A&M (10), UCLA (7), and Texas Tech (5). This roster is either going to look insane by the Sweet 16 or be completely eliminated by Saturday. The Furman pick at **15-seed** is the single biggest Cinderella bet in the entire draft. Alex Wilkins, you carry the hopes and dreams of Newman1623 on your shoulders.

### The "Sleeper of the Draft" Award -- Bennett Stirtz (Scouter10, Pick #61)
20.0 PPG and 37.5 MPG on a 9-seed Iowa. This guy plays the most minutes of anyone drafted AND scores 20 a game, and he went in ROUND 7. If Iowa pulls the 8/9 upset, Stirtz could be the steal of the entire draft.

### The "Stacker" Award -- ian14218
Two Arizona Wildcats with picks #3 and #18, plus two Houston Cougars later on. ian14218 is going all-in on teams going deep. If Arizona and Houston both make the Final Four, this roster is going to be disgusting. If either one gets bounced early, well, that's a lot of eggs in a broken basket.
